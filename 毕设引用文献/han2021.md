Abstract: With the rapid development of computer technology, artificial intelligence (AI) has been widely used in the field of art design. This paper mainly analyzes the impact and cases of artificial intelligence on art design, and it demonstrates that under the promotion of science and technology, the application of artificial intelligence in the field of art design can open up a more intelligent and personalized development direction for future design. The combination of artificial intelligence and art design makes the thinking mode of art design more diversified so that the future art design can better serve all mankind. 

Key words: artificial intelligence, art design, collaboration, application, fashion industry 

# I. INTRODUCTION

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has reached a peak of research and development. The computing power of AI doubles every half year, and the literature of AI doubles every 18 months. New methods, hardware, and applications are emerging. AI has also been widely used in the field of art design, which bringing designers and consumers a more diverse and intelligent experience. There are boundless possibilities for future development of art design. 

# II. THE INFLUENCE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ON ART DESIGN

# A. Artificial Intelligence

In 1956, Dartmouth College held the first artificial intelligence conference, which put forward the concept of artificial intelligence (AI). It lays a foundation for the early research of "structure simulation" and "function simulation". Structure simulation is to realize AI by simulating the structure of human cerebral cortex neural network, that is, to simulate human thinking ability by constructing Artificial Neural Network (ANN). Function simulation is a way to reflect the functional characteristics and laws of research objects by simulating human thinking. Because computer simulation of human thinking process is actually a modeling process, how to find efficient model structure is the key to construct decision model. 

AI is a new technology which researches and develops the theory, method, technology and application system for simulating, extending and expanding human intelligence. The purpose of developing AI is to explore the essence of intelligence, develop intelligent machines with humanlike intelligence, extend and enhance the ability and efficiency of human beings in various tasks of changing nature and governing society. In recent years, artificial intelligence has developed rapidly, and application examples emerge endlessly. 

The application of AI in image generation provides a good bridge for the introduction of AI into the design field. Ian Goodfellow et al. has proposed the Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) in 2014, which can generate better output by mutual game learning between discriminator and generator, and can be better used in image generation, semantic segmentation and other tasks. The purpose of image generation is to generate new images that are not in the original training data set through learning, which can be regarded as the process of creation while semantic segmentation creates convenience for designers to extract design elements. Based on this possibility, researchers at the forefront of science have tried to use AI in the field of art and design. For example, Qian Dong, Chen Yingqiao, Huang Si and other graduate students from the research and development team of Software School of Zhejiang University developed "Xiang Ding - artificial intelligence cultural and creative product customization service platform". Based on the new AI technology, the platform provides a whole process solution of personalized design, customization and sales service for consumers and enterprises of the cultural and tourism industry and designers. Till now, the team has developed traditional culture style, festival style, world famous painting style and independent artist co-branding style for the cultural and tourism industry. The team is committed to bringing top level art into public life and providing IP conversion platform for independent artists. Among them, with the AI Postcard personalized customization service, users can choose any digital photos to combine with the artist style provided by the platform. # B. The development of art design

From the perspective of the development of human history, human expressed their needs and innovation through design, and the results of design inevitably reflect the progress, development and expectation of the culture. The appearance of culture can be seen and explained by design. We can use design as a window and indicator to understand an era, a country and a region. The development of design can not only reflect the culture of the times, but also the trend and essence of the development of the culture. With the development of the times, there is an increasing trend of cross integration between natural sciences and humanities and social sciences, and the relationship between art and science and technology is also concerned. 

For art design, technology is a fundamental element. For example, the foundation of digital media art is digital technology, which mainly uses modern computer and network information technology to edit and process optical signals and sound signals, and then convert them into digital information 

for storage and management. Fashion design is based on the pattern, structure, color, fabric and so on. These design links can be included in the technical category strictly. Therefore, art design itself has scientific connotation. 

With the development of computer technology at the age of AI, many designers around the world are exploring the new design method based on AI. It can be said that the integration of AI into art design is inevitable. 

# C. Artificial intelligence and art design

Technology is a tool which can't solve all the design problems. At present, artwork is considered as a field that AI cannot be completely replaced by AI. This is because art is the expression of the artist's emotions and the crystallization of the artist's thoughts, while machines do not have human emotions and cannot think actively. At present, most AI is still at the level of "weak AI". AI has experienced the process from "shallow learning" to "deep learning". With the development of "deep learning", AI has gradually improved the ability to understand and recognize things. It can understand semantics quickly and accurately, and extract all information. This is a very big development in the history of AI. With the rapid increase of available data, efficient cloud computing capabilities, and more powerful algorithms, the development of AI is accelerating. # III. APPLICATION OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN ART DESIGN

# A. The reflect of artificial intelligence in painting and design

The 18-year-old AI Girl "Xiaobing", officially launched by Microsoft (Asia) Internet Engineering Institute in May 2014, is a complete AI underlying framework that combines natural language processing, computer voice and computer vision technologies. It is a complete set of basic framework of AI interaction subject oriented to the whole process of interaction, also called the Avatar Framework. It includes a core dialog engine, multiple interactive senses, triggering of third-party content and first-party content generation, and cross-platform deployment solutions. This framework focuses on the development of AI in fitting the dimension of human EQ and emphasizes the basic value of EQ of AI in human-machine interaction. 

Xiaobing uses AI to create technology and to learn the ability of excellent human creators, to generate content based on text, voice and vision. At present, it is not only a poet, a musician, but also a painter and designer. As an artist, in May 2019, Xiaobing graduated from the Central Academy of Fine Arts under the alias of "Xia Yubing", and held an exhibition "Alternative Worlds" in the Central Academy of Fine Arts Art Gallery in July. This is the result of its 22-month study of the paintings of 236 famous human painters in the 400-year art history of human society using GANs models. 

# B. Application of artificial intelligence in fashion industry

With the continuous expansion of intellectualization in the application field of fashion industry, AI has gradually become a major tool for innovation in textile and fashion industry. 

Relying on AI, the design ability of Xiaobing has been realized in the fields of textile and fashion fabric design, jewelry accessories design, digital printing pattern design. Xiaobing can 

design more than ten mainstream styles stably and achieve on-demand creation. The design diversity of its design reaches $10^{26}$ . During the China International Textile Fabrics and Accessories Expo in September 2018, the China Textile Information Center conjunction with Microsoft (Asia) Internet Engineering Institute has transformed the 2019/20AW trend studied by China Textile Information Center into textile and fashion printing and jacquard pattern through the emotional computing framework of Microsoft Xiaobing, and the base enterprises of Chinese national textile product development has produced several printed and jacquard products designed by Xiaobing. The textiles designed by Xiaobing, as well as the neckties and shirts designed with this fabric, have been permanently collected by China Silk Museum. In addition, in cooperation with Wensli, relying on AI technology, Xiaobing team has completed the deployment of the customized silk scarf design and retail platform "Xihu No.1", which has been officially launched in all retail stores of Wensli in Hangzhou. The platform can realize the real-time communication between AI designer Xiaobing and consumers, and it can carry out one-to-one customized design and production of silk scarves according to the characteristics of consumers, which can meet the personalized needs of each consumer and greatly reduce the operating costs of manufacturing and retail enterprises. 

# C. Generation of the intelligent digital content in fashion industry

As a big country in the global textile and fashion industry, China's fashion industry is undergoing a transformation. In recent years, the clothing consumption demand of China has turned from single functional to a diverse one including cultural and trending brand. The fashion industry is facing the pressure of transformation and upgrading, the scale of the industry is declining, and the demand for science and technology is strong. The promotion of AI is exerting an increasing influence on the fashion industry. On the one hand, there will be more scientific and technological talents participating in fashion design. On the other hand, fashion brands can also use intelligent analysis technology to detect market sales data to improve production efficiency and profits. 

At present, some technology companies based on big data and artificial intelligence are emerging in the market to serve the fashion industry. Among them, Beijing Shenshang Technology Co., Ltd. (Styling AI) uses AI technology to monitor global fashion trends through self-built big data. It collects fashion information of global major shows, fashion brands, cross-border e-commerce, domestic and foreign social media (Instagram, xiaohongshu.com). At present, it has achieved a comprehensive coverage of the four international fashion weeks, with detection data of more than 1000 international brands, more than 1 million Instagram fashion bloggers, more than 100000 Tmall Taobao brands. The company has more than 100 million fashion pictures and more than 10 billion fashion data in stock. Styling AI has established a complete artificial intelligence model library, and the accuracy of the model generally exceeds $90\%$ . Using GANs technology, Styling AI has developed an AI Model Library, as shown in figure 4. At present, it has more than 10000 AI models, covering three categories of men, women and children. Asian, white and black people are included. For the models in each AI 

model library, the combination of different expressions and different postures developed by Styling AI can be used by various industries.

# IV. FUTURE DEVELOPMENT OF ART DESIGN

# A. For the common well-being of mankind

In the second half of the 19th century, the design level of mass industrial production after the industrial revolution decreased, which gave birth to the Arts & Crafts Movement originated in England. Under the leadership of artist and poet William Morris, artists and designers at that time tried to escape the reality and revive the handicraft tradition in the middle ages because they could not solve the problems brought by industrialization. In order to deal with the opportunities and challenges brought by machines, Bauhaus was born one hundred years ago. Now, a hundred years later, we are facing the design revolution and challenges brought by AI. People have strong expectations for the coming of the era of AI, but also full of worries. What kind of changes AI will bring to human society is full of uncertainty. Machines are changing our way of life and living environment just as they were a hundred years ago. At the same time, they also promote the continuous development of society. 

At present, AI has been able to achieve the preliminary imitation and creation in the field such as painting, poetry, design, music that human need to use their own sensibility to experience and make done. As Professor Li Feifei of Stanford University once said: "We are now on the top wave of the age of AI. The scope and scale of the AI era will be more profound than any other transition period in our history. AI has the potential to fundamentally change every industry and every society. Therefore, the creators and designers of AI must widely represent human beings. We are venturing into a new world that is being built. As creators of this new technology, it is our collective responsibility to guide AI to have a positive impact on our planet, our country, our communities, our families and our lives." So based on the change of design tools, the improvement of design efficiency, the change of design thinking mode, and the change of industry, contemporary designers must consider the usability of design products in addition to the functionality and artistry of products as well as the sustainable development of the world, the social ethics of the products and whether it represents the common well-being of mankind. 

# B. Cooperation between artificial intelligence and human brain

The participation of computer, software and 3D technology has greatly shortened the product design cycle. It improves the production efficiency, creates great humanistic and economic value. The integration of art design and science is inseparable 

from the cooperation of AI and human brain. We should clearly recognize that the intervention of rational thinking of technology in design is inevitable, but it has great uncertainty. In the process of design, we still need excellent designers to properly grasp the rational design thinking through leading technical training, so as to complete better design. In many future jobs, the higher the degree of machine participation, the lower the degree of human brain participation. However, in art design, due to the requirements of design disciplines for aesthetics, culture, history, philosophy and other disciplines, the participation of human brain will not be decreased, but collaborate more. Modern tools based on information technology help us save a lot of time, but in the expression of people's intention, many problems are still related to people's quality, especially aesthetic quality. As Laszlo Moholy Nagy, the most outstanding designer of the 20th century, once wrote in his book New World: "It's not against technological progress, but how to use them for the benefit of all. Man can get freedom through technology, as long as he can finally realize his function, that is, to achieve a balanced life by making full use of the released energy." 

# V. CONCLUSION

At present, the deep integration of AI and art design in the world is still in different stages and forms. The combination of artificial intelligence and art design not only improves the efficiency of art design, but also makes the thinking mode of art design more diversified. Artificial intelligence not only promotes the continuous integration of science and art, but also enables today's designers to think deeper on how to develop future design. The integrated development of computer technology, artificial intelligence and art design will create a better world for the common well-being of mankind.
